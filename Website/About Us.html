The Motive of this website














As we all know that due to Corona virus (Covid-19), the government had shut down the schools and colleges across the country temporarily. As per present situation, there is uncertainty. However, every cloud has a silver lining. We are extremely thankful to our respected principal madam and teachers for introducing us to introducing us to the technology based education. Technology based education rely on digitization. The ed-tech industry is flourishing. The lockdown resulted in the closing of schools an colleges, students and teachers alike had turned compu-savey platforms to help in maintaining the academic momentum. There is always two faces of every coin. E-learning too has it's fair share of pros. and cons.. Due to flexibility timings and time management skills, as well as link management,proper schedule and plannings needs to be set and followed to maintaining the momentum. One of the biggest problem faced in this ed-tech learning is the problem of internet connectivity and link connectivity.

So,to sought out these problems, we have created a unique website under the able guidance of Srikanth sir, which is free of cost.in which time table of all the classes with HTML (HYPER TEXT MARKUP LANGUAGE). In this website, the links are linked with the periods, so if the students click on any period, they will be directly land to the "ask to join the meeting" page and if teachers click their respective periods, they will be also directly land to the "start the meeting" page and with this, teachers won't have to send the links in the groups.

OUR DEVELOPMENT TEAM MEMBERS are: Dewanshu Dewangan, Paras Singh, Pratyush Jha, Shahina Parveen, and Soumya Sharaf.

HTML programing can be done in variety of devices using different softwares. Some Softwares we've used are as follows-: (1) Android: Anwriter Free HTML programer, Webmaster; (2) Windows: VS code, Atom;

All this was seem to be impossible to us without the teachings and able guidance of Srikanth sir. We also want to thank Krishna sir to helping us in completing this introduction.
